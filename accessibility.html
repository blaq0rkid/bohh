Accessibility Statement

Commitment to Inclusion and Dignity

Black Orchid Honor House (BOHH) is dedicated to ensuring that our digital presence is accessible to everyone, reflecting our core mission of empowerment and honor-based community building. We believe that information and support services should be available to all individuals, regardless of ability.

1. Standards and Goals

We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of bohh.org. Our goal is to conform to the World Wide Web Consortium's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with a wide array of disabilities.

2. Ongoing Efforts

We view accessibility as an ongoing commitment. We regularly review our site to identify and fix potential barriers. Our efforts include:

  • Providing alternative text for images.
  • Ensuring keyboard-only navigation.
  • Maintaining high-contrast color schemes for readability.
  • Structuring content with clear headings for screen reader compatibility.

3. Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

Black Orchid Honor House is currently working toward full conformance with WCAG 2.1 level AA. While we strive for complete accessibility, some parts of our content may be in the process of being optimized as we update our legacy systems and digital resources.

4. Technical Specifications

Accessibility of bohh.org relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

5. Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of Black Orchid Honor House's website, there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations and potential solutions:

Third-Party Content: Some content or integrated tools provided by third parties (e.g., social media feeds or external maps) may not fully meet WCAG standards. We actively encourage our partners to provide accessible content.

PDF Documents: Older documents may not be fully optimized for screen readers. We are in the process of converting critical documents into accessible web formats.

If you encounter an issue, please contact us using the information below, and we will provide the information in an alternative format.

6. Feedback and Contact Information

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Black Orchid Honor House. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers while trying to access our programs or services:

Email: contact@bohh.org

Website: bohh.org/contact

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days.

7. Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility inquiry, you may reach out to our administration to escalate the matter. We are committed to a process of continuous improvement and will work diligently to resolve any identified barriers.
